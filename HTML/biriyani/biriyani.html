Chennai Thalapakatti Biriyani

    Ingridents

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Mint

    Mutton Biriyani Making Process

  1. ½ kg mutton (cleaned)
  2. 2 cups basmati rice
  3. 3 onions (sliced)
  4. 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  5. ¾ cup curd
  6. 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  7. 3–4 green chillies
  8. 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  9. 1 tbsp biryani masala
  10. ½ tsp turmeric
  11. Mint and coriander leaves
  12. Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  13. Oil and Ghee and Salt
